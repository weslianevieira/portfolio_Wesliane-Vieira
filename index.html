About

More about me

My name is Wesliane Vieira and I am a web developer. I have experience in a variety of coding languages and software, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Material UI, and React.

At a pivotal moment in my life, I developed a deep appreciation for technology. During a period of prolonged illness, I was seeking an outlet that could distract me from the difficult reality. Fortunately, I discovered the dynamic and imaginative world of technology, which provided me with an escape from my struggles and the motivation to remain hopeful and active.

I have worked on projects both independently and in teams, and am always looking for opportunities to expand my knowledge and skill set. Throughout my career, I have strived to develop quality web applications that are both effective and user-friendly. I am also constantly looking to improve my skills and learn more about the latest web development technologies.

I hope you find this portfolio useful in seeing what I can do and how I approach web development. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to discuss a potential project.

Thank you for your time and interest!
